About Our Company

Our commitment resides in manufacturing superior charcoal briquettes while simultaneously developing cutting-edge molding technology that empowers other businesses to succeed in the charcoal industry.

Company Brief Profile

Our company, established in Bekasi in 2026, operates in the field of manufacturing automatic charcoal briquette machines. It was founded with the vision of transforming low-value agricultural waste—particularly rice husk and conventional charcoal—into high-value products that are more practical, eco-friendly, and marketable. By focusing on innovation and automation, the company aims to support sustainable energy solutions, empower local communities, and create new business opportunities. Although still in its early stages, the company is committed to driving efficiency in briquette production and contributing to both economic growth and environmental sustainability.
